History

In 1995, a small group of passionate adventurers came together to explore the untamed rivers of the Pacific Northwest. With nothing more than a few old rafts and a love for the outdoors, they set out on a mission to provide others with the thrill of rafting. The first trip was a modest success, with just a handful of clients, but it ignited a fire that would grow into something much bigger. As the years went by, word of mouth spread, and the company expanded its offerings to include more challenging routes and more luxurious trips. By 2005, we were the go-to rafting company in the region, offering both serene float trips and high-adrenaline whitewater adventures for people of all ages.

Today, Fantastic Squirt Rafting is a leader in adventure tourism, offering unforgettable experiences not only on the rapids of the Pacific Northwest but around the world. Our team of expert guides, who have years of experience and a deep love for nature, are dedicated to ensuring that every trip is safe, fun, and filled with adventure. Our mission has always been to make people’s dreams of adventure come true, whether it’s navigating raging rivers or quietly drifting through scenic landscapes. With an eye on sustainability and preserving the environments we explore, we aim to continue providing world-class rafting experiences for generations to come.
